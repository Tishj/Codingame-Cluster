This is a league based challenge.

For this challenge, multiple leagues for the same game are available. Once you have proven yourself against the first Boss, you will access a higher league and extra rules will be available.

  Goal

Connect 4+> chips to win before your opponent.
Decide between placing chips at strategic positions, or to rotate the board to a different side.

  Rules

The game takes place on a hexagonal grid representing the board.
The players take turns to take actions.

Sides

The board is rotated to a side:
  • Side 0 the board is leaning on its SOUTH side. (default)
  • Side 1 the board is leaning on its SOUTH WEST side.
  • Side 2 the board is leaning on its NORTH WEST side.
  • Side 3 the board is leaning on its NORTH side.
  • Side 4 the board is leaning on its NORTH EAST side.
  • Side 5 the board is leaning on its SOUTH EAST side.
The board starts of on the SOUTH side. (wont be changed this league)
ROTATE commands can be used to change the side by amount, coming back to 0 after passing 5.
When chips fall, they will travel over the board according to side that the board is leaning on.

Board

The board is made up of hexagonal cells, arranged to form a larger hexagon.
Each cell has an index and up to six neighbors.
Each direction is labelled 0 to 5.
Hex sides

Columns

Chips can only be inserted onto cells at the top of the board.
At the start of the game, these cells are given to you in the numberOfColumns.
These cells can also be identified as the only cells with -1 as neighbour for that side.

Chips

Every player has a bag containing their chips, at the start of each round 2 chips are selected from this bag.
At the start of a turn, the player receives 2 chips from their bag.
Then the player can place one of these chips on the board.
When making a DROP action, the other selected chips go back into the bag.
When a ROTATE action is made, the selected chips are destroyed.

Chips will fall downwards according to the side the board is leaning on.

Actions

The players take turns performing actions, the player can perform one of two possible actions.
The possible actions are:
  • DROP cell color: Insert a chip of a specific color at a cell.
  • ROTATE amount: Rotate the board clock-wise by a certain amount of cycles (1-5), changing the side.

Drop action

When performing a drop action, you reduce your amount of remaining chips for that color by 1.
The other selected chips go back into the bag.
You are only allowed to drop onto cells that were given in numberOfColumns for that side.
You may not drop onto a cell that currently contains a chip.
You can only drop a color that has been selected for you for that round.

Rotate action

When performing a rotate action, you destroy all of the chips that were selected for you at the start of the round. (reducing the amount remaining)
You may only rotate by 1-5(inclusive) cycles.
Chips will "fall" according to the new side the board is leaning on.

The game lasts at most 60 rounds or until one player has run out of chips to place.
Victory Conditions
    The winner is the player with the highest score, once at least one player has reached 200 score.
Defeat Conditions
    Your program does not provide a command in the allotted time or it provides an unrecognized/invalid command.

  Technical Details

  • Chips are initialized with the formula:
    (numberOfCells / 2) - ((numberOfCells / 2) % 2
  • Chip connections are prioritized by:
    • Size of the connection.
    • Amount of chips of that type placed on the board.
  • You can check out the source code of this game on this GitHub repo.

  Game Protocol

Initialization Input
First line: numberOfCells --always 60 for this league.
Next numberOfCells lines: 7 space-separated integers:
  • index for the cell's index.
  • 6 neigh variables, one for each side, containing the index of a neighboring cell or -1 if there is no neighbor.
Next line: numberOfColumns.
Next numberOfColumns lines: 6 space-separated integers:
  • 6 cell variables, one for each side, containing the index of the cell at this position for every side.
Next line: yourColors.
Next yourColors lines: 1 integer:
  • maxAmount for the total amount of chips of this color in your bag.
Next line: opponentColors.
Next opponentColors lines: 1 integer:
  • maxAmount for the total amount of chips of this color in their bag.
Input for One Game Turn
First line: An integer side: the current side the board is leaning on. (between 0 and 5) --ignore for this league
Next line: numberOfValidColumns.
Next numberOfValidColumns lines: 1 integer:
  • cellIndex: a cell that can be used with a DROP action for this turn.
    guaranteed to not contain a chip on this turn.
Next line: numberOfChips.
Next numberOfChips lines: 4 space-separated integers:
  • index: the index of the new chip.
  • colorIndex: the color of the new chip.
  • isMine: 1 if you are the owner of this chip.
  • cellIndex: the index of the cell the chip is currently on.
Next line: numberOfColorsInHand.
Next numberOfColorsInHand lines: 1 integer:
  • colorIndex: a color selected from the bag this round.
Output
A single line with your command:
  • DROP cell color: drop a chip of type color onto the board at cell.
  • ROTATE amount: Rotate the board clock-wise by a certain amount of cycles (1-5), changing the side.
